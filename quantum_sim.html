Quantum Particle Interaction Visualization

This visualization simulates a small collection of "quantum" particles interacting with one another. Each particle has a positive (red) or negative (blue) charge, and forces between them are calculated using a simple Coulomb‑like law. When particles attract or repel, the lines connecting them illustrate the invisible exchange of force‑carrier bosons. The simulation is not a rigorous quantum calculation but an educational representation of how particles interact at the subatomic level.
